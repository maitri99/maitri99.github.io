Air Quality Index in United States

May 25, 2021| Category: Data Visualization

The data for this project has been imported from the US EPA website. You can find Zip archives of csv files for each parameter of criteria gases. These gases are all contained in the AQI defined pollutants class of parameters. I downlaoded the features of data for each of Carbon Monoxide (CO), Ozone (O3), Sulfur Dioxide (SO2), and Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) from 2010 to 2018.

Import the necessary libraries such as Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, clean and organize the data, do the necessary groupby of the columns, and plot the lineplots of desired states.

Let's look at the Average AQI in Alabama State.

We can also take a look at the partial Autocorrelation plots for different gases. Partial autocorrelation plots are commonly used for model identification. These plots can help in identifying whether an Autoregressive model is appropriate for the data or if an AR model is appropriate, then what order should we use? Here we are just concerned with the general trends in air quality so, the order doesn't really matter.
